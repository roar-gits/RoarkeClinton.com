Roarke's ATA Recurring Monthly Retainer Subscription V1

Role
Flexible Tech Support - PM, Design, & Development.

Goal
‍This retainer is meant to provide ATA and Roarke with an expectation of how much effort Roarke will commit to ATA during the month.

Retainer Breakdown
This recurring monthly retainer subscription contract is $440/month ($880/month minus in-kind donations) + payment processing fees, and is to be paid on the first of every month.  The retainer pays for about 16 hours of work, set at $55/hour, spread over the coming month, and meant to allocate approximately 4 hours of work per week. If retainer hours are exceeded during the month, Roarke will alert Cailyn & Laurie, at Aloha Tree Alliance "Client," to get approval and will bill the Client for extra hours worked at the end of the month.

Agreement
By paying this Retainer Subscription ATA agrees that:
• ATA is paying Roarke for focused effort (work hours) to achieve ATA's goals, and ATA understands that while Roarke will do his best to achieve those goals no specific outcome is guaranteed.
• ATA accepts responsibility for defining priorities and timelines for Roarke, even if the ask is to have Roarke help ATA define priorities and timelines.
