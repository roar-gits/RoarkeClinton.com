Overview and Objectives

Client seeks Consultant’s support in managing Client's assets, projects, and initiatives, and in creating and running a management system to optimize ongoing initiatives. This Agreement establishes an engagement where Client may subscribe, as needed, to Consultant’s valuable service through monthly service packages. The Agreement is designed to provide flexible and scalable support, predictable planning, and a strategic partnership leveraging Consultant’s growing understanding of Client’s needs

‍

1. Term and Engagement

1.1 Agreement Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues indefinitely unless terminated in accordance with Section 8 (Termination).

1.2 Engagement Model
This Agreement operates on a forward-looking subscription basis, allowing the Client to:
    (a) Subscribe to Consultant's professional services in monthly packages, detailed in Section 3 (Service Packages)
    (b) Adjust or pause subscriptions as Client's needs evolve per Section 3.2 (Package Selection and Changes), without renegotiating the entire Agreement
    (c) Maintain continuity during months when no subscription is active per Section 4.2(e) (i.e., the Agreement is still in force, but no services are delivered or fees incurred)
    (d) Benefit from Consultant's growing familiarity with Client's priorities over time, as described in Section 2.3 (Partnership Evolution)

1.3 Service Period
Each Service Period is one calendar month, for which the Client subscribes to the Consultant’s services (as needed). If the Client chooses not to subscribe for a particular month, no fees apply, but the Agreement remains in effect.

1.4 Capacity Limits
Consultant's overall service capacity per Service Period is shared among all clients, with specific package limits detailed in Section 3 (Service Packages). This ensures that each subscribing client receives consistent, high-quality service within their selected package's scope.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 Professional Services
Consultant shall provide professional services in support of Client's personal and business endeavors, adapting as Client's needs evolve. Services include proactive support and strategic guidance in any areas where Consultant can add value, subject to:
      (a) Capacity limits in Section 3.1 (Available Packages)
      (b) Delivery terms in Section 4 (Service Delivery)
      (c) Subscription terms in Section 5.1 (Subscriptions)
      (d) Limitations and disclaimers in Section 9.2 (Limitation of Liability)

2.2 Service Scope and Approach
Consultant has sole discretion to determine the appropriate mix and scope of services based on:
      (a) Client's current needs and priorities
      (b) Consultant's professional judgment and expertise
      (c) Opportunities for optimization and improvement
      (d) Available capacity within the selected Package per Section 3.1
      (e) Practical considerations for effective delivery per Section 4 (Service Delivery)
      (f) Service limitations and disclaimers per Section 9.2

2.3 Partnership Evolution
The Parties acknowledge that:
      (a) The engagement's value arises from both:
            - Consultant's ability to provide effective services
            - Client's constructive engagement in the relationship
      (b) Service value increases as Consultant develops deeper insight into Client's needs and goals
      (c) Services evolve as new opportunities for optimization are identified

2.4 Service Flexibility
Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as:
      (a) Requiring Consultant to provide any specific services
      (b) Limiting the types of services Consultant may provide
      (c) Creating obligations to perform specific tasks in any given month

2.5 Service Commitment
The Consultant commits to:
      (a) Proactive communication about opportunities and challenges
      (b) Regular check-ins to ensure alignment with Client's needs
      (c) Continuous improvement of service delivery
      (d) Flexibility in adapting to Client's changing requirements

4. SERVICE DELIVERY

4.1 Business Hours
      (a) Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time
      (b) Federal holidays observed
      (c) Off-hours: any time outside business hours, including weekends and observed holidays
      (d) Off-hours service delivery and rates governed by Section 3.1 (Available Packages) and Section 4.5 for travel services

4.2 Service Availability and Continuity
      (a) Services provided during business hours, as scheduled and at Consultant's sole discretion
      (b) Off-hours support is available pursuant to package terms per Section 3.1, solely at Consultant's availability and discretion
      (c) While Consultant generally processes requests in the order received, Consultant maintains absolute discretion over all aspects of service delivery, including:
            - Accepting or declining service requests
            - Scheduling and prioritizing work
            - Implementation timing and approach
            - Resource allocation across clients
      (d) Services may be suspended without valid payment per Section 5.1
      (e) Client may pause services with notice as specified in Section 3.2(b)
      (f) Agreement remains active during pauses per Section 1.2(c)

4.3 Communication
      (a) Communication methods (e.g., email, messaging) will be determined by mutual agreement
      (b) Official notices must follow Section 9.8 (Official Notices and Communication)
      (c) Response expected during business hours only
      (d) Off-hours communication is at Consultant's discretion

4.4 Capacity Management
      (a) Time Monitoring
            - Consultant tracks service time in 30-minute increments for capacity management
            - Includes time spent on administrative tasks and client communications
            - No rollover of unused capacity to subsequent months      (b) Capacity Updates
            - Consultant notifies Client upon reaching 75% of monthly package capacity
            - Capacity status updates available upon Client request
      (c) Capacity Exhaustion
            - Services may pause when monthly package capacity is reached
            - Additional services beyond package capacity at Consultant's discretion
      (d) Service Request Timing and Feasibility
            - Monthly package capacity represents maximum available service hours, not guaranteed utilization
            - Service delivery requires reasonable notice and scheduling at Consultant's sole discretion per Section 4.2
            - Package fees are not prorated or refunded under any circumstances, including but not limited to:
- Unutilized capacity
- Late-period or unfeasible requests
- Client's choice not to utilize services
- Consultant's exercise of discretion per Section 2.2
            - Consultant may defer completion of requests to next Service Period if insufficient capacity remains for proper execution
            - Service timing and execution priorities determined by Consultant based on:
- Client priorities
- Order in which Client(s) requests are received
- Request complexity
- Available resources
- Other active commitments
- Required preparation time

4.5 Business Travel Services
      (a) Travel Planning and Coordination
            - Travel arrangements subject to Consultant's availability
            - Travel subject to Consultant's discretion
            - Travel expenses managed per Section 5.3 (Business Expenses)
            - Client approval required for all travel arrangements
      (b) Travel Time Compensation
            - Travel time billed at $150 per hour, separate from package subscription per Section 5.1
            - Minimum of 4 hours, maximum of 8 hours billed per travel day
            - Actual services provided during travel count against package hours per Section 3.1
            - Travel time includes:
- Transit to/from airports or stations
- Flight or transit duration
- Required waiting periods
- Required layovers
      (c) On-Location Business Days
            - Days spent at business destination count against package hours per Section 3.1
            - Standard daily maximum of 8 package hours
            - Services provided beyond 8 hours subject to off-hours policy per Section 3.1
            - Weekends and holidays at destination follow standard off-hours terms      
            - Partial days (arrival/departure) counted based on actual service time
      (d) General Travel Terms
            - Changes to travel schedule subject to Consultant's availability
            - Actual services provided during travel time count against package hours
            - Client may request specific working hours while on location
            - Non-working meals and rest periods excluded from package hours

5. PAYMENT TERMS

5.1 Subscriptions
      (a) Each Service Period aligns with the calendar month, as defined 1.3 Service Period
      (b) Packages auto-renew monthly at the same level unless changed prior to billing in the week before the upcoming Service Period
      (c) Once the Service Period begins, subscription fees are non-refundable
      (d) Subscription Status
            - Active status and changes tracked through payments and pause notices
            - No separate documentation required beyond payment records and pause notices

5.2 Ad-hoc Package Additions
      (a) Ad-hoc packages may be purchased during an active Service Period
      (b) Payment for ad-hoc packages is:
            - Due at time of purchase
            - Non-refundable
            - Separate from regular subscription billing
      (c) Ad-hoc packages do not auto-renew

5.3 Payment Process
      (a) Service Subscription
            - Client authorizes Consultant to charge the approved payment method
            - Simple monthly the week before the upcoming service period
            - Flexibility to adjust package until billing date per Section 3.2
            - Convenient payment options:
                        • ACH transfer (preferred)
                        • Wire transfer
                        • Credit card
      (b) Expense Reimbursement
            - Separate from subscription payments
            - Previous month's expenses invoiced by end o the month following the service period
            - Payment due within 15 days of expense invoice, surviving termination per Section 8.4
            - May be paid via same or different payment method than subscription

5.4 Business Expenses
      (a) Expense Coverage
            - Client responsible for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in performing services
            - Consultant commits to managing expenses with same care as if personally incurring them
            - Including but not limited to:
                        • Transportation and parking
                        • Meeting and event costs
                        • Supplies and materials
                        • Subscriptions and services
                        • Travel expenses (transportation, lodging, meals, incidentals)
      (b) Expense Management
            - Consultant will seek cost-effective solutions while maintaining service quality
            - Client's existing vendor relationships and preferences will be utilized when available
            - Consultant will avoid unnecessary or excessive expenses
            - Bulk purchases or subscriptions require discussion of alternatives and Client approval
      (c) Approval Requirements
            - Business necessary expenses under $100 don't require pre-approval
            - Expenses over $100 require Client approval
            - All major travel expenses require advance approval per Section 4.5
            - Client may specify preferred vendors or parameters
      (d) Documentation
            - Monthly expense report covers previous month's expenses
            - All expenses documented as digitized receipts
            - Expense reports and receipts submitted with expense invoice per Section 5.3(b)
            - Client may request additional expense documentation

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Definition
“Confidential Information” means any non-public information related to Client’s personal, business, or financial affairs to which Consultant gains access while providing services.

6.2 Protection Obligations
Consultant shall:
      (a) Maintain strict confidentiality of Client information
      (b) Use such information solely to provide services
      (c) Implement reasonable security measures
      (d) Not disclose information to third parties without Client’s written consent

6.3 Exceptions
Confidentiality does not apply if the information:
      (a) Becomes public through no fault of Consultant
      (b) Is received from a third party who is not under any confidentiality obligation
      (c) Must be disclosed by law or court order

6.4 Duration
All confidentiality obligations survive the termination of this Agreement indefinitely.
